Our Journey

From humble beginnings to becoming a competitive youth team.

2018

Foundation

Thunderbolts FC was founded by coach Michael Thompson with just 7 local boys passionate about football. Training began at the community park with minimal equipment.

2019

First Tournament

Entered our first local youth tournament, finishing 4th out of 8 teams. This experience showed our potential and areas needing improvement.

2020

Sponsorship & Growth

Secured our first sponsor, allowing for proper kits and training equipment. Expanded to a full squad of 16 players with structured training sessions.

2021

League Champions

Won the U14 Regional Youth League, our first major trophy. Finished the season unbeaten with 12 wins and 2 draws, scoring 38 goals.

2022-Present

State Level Competition

Competing at state level tournaments, with several players being scouted by academy teams. Continued development of our youth program with a focus on education and football.
